Project Overview

This Retail Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of overall business performance in the retail sector. The analysis includes key performance metrics, monthly sales trends, product category breakdowns, store type comparisons, and customer segmentation. The dashboard is designed to help businesses understand their growth, optimize strategies,and make informed decisions based on historical and current sales data.

Key Findings

  • Total Sales & Profitability:

    • The business achieved $3.01 million in total sales and $858.8K in profit, resulting in a profit margin of 28.51% — indicating healthy profitability.

  • Customer & Order Metrics:

    • The dataset includes 1,500 customers and approximately 27,000 orders, reflecting a solid and active customer base.

  • Monthly Sales Trends (2023-2025):

    • The sales trend chart shows seasonal peaks in November, likely due to holiday shopping. There is a consistent year-over-year increase with some expected fluctuations, confirming long-term business growth.

  • Product Category Breakdown:

    • Top-performing categories include:

    • Apparel & Fashion - $0.40M
    • Tools & Hardware - $0.27M
    • Toys & Games - $0.17M
    • Others (Stationery, Health & Beauty, etc.) contribute smaller shares

  • Store Type Comparison:

    • Sales distribution by channel shows:

    • Outlet stores dominate with $2.0M in revenue
    • Online sales - $0.7M
    • Flagship stores - smaller share

  • Customer Segmentation:

    • Loyalty-based customer breakdown:

    • New customers - 52.2%
    • Regular customers - 37.53%
    • VIP customers - 10.27%

  • Geographic Sales Distribution:

    • A choropleth map of the U.S. reveals stronger sales performance in the Midwest and East Coast, suggesting potential regions for expansion or focused marketing.

Conclusion

The analysis provides a 360-degree view of the business's retail performance. The consistent growth in sales, combined with customer segmentation and product breakdowns, empowers the company to make strategic decisions on inventory, marketing, and store operations. Seasonal insights and regional differences also provide a competitive edge in resource allocation and customer targeting.

Dashboard Design & Interactivity

To ensure a user-friendly and insightful experience, I designed this dashboard with the following interactive features:

  • One Main Overview Page: All key metrics and summaries are shown on the main page to provide a high-level snapshot of business performance.
  • Drill-Through Functionality:

    • The main dashboard enables users to right-click and navigate to four detailed drill-through pages focused on:

    • Sales Performance
    • Product Insights
    • Store Performance
    • Customer Analysis

  • Drill-down functionality (e.g., Year → Month) in trend visuals
  • Custom DAX measures, Calculated Columns for calculating profit margin, YoY growth, and segmentation
  • Clean, responsive layout with tooltips for enhanced user experience
